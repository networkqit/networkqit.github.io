networkqit.algorithms.optimize.Adam¶

class Adam(G: autograd.numpy.numpy_wrapper.array, x0: autograd.numpy.numpy_wrapper.array, model: networkqit.graphtheory.models.GraphModel.GraphModel, **kwargs)[source]¶

Implements the ADAM stochastic gradient descent. Adam: A Method for Stochastic Optimization Diederik P. Kingma, Jimmy Ba

https://arxiv.org/abs/1412.6980 However here we use quasi-hyperbolic adam by default, with parameters nu1,nu2 https://arxiv.org/pdf/1810.06801.pdf
